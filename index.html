Crystal Calculator

Hi there~ Strawberry Cookie is very shy, but I happen to know that she's also very smart. If you're willing to be her friend, she will calculate how strong your Crystal Engine is for you.

All you have to do is go through each of the tables below and tell her which treasures you have, and what level they are. Any changes will automatically trigger a recalculation, and you can see the result at the bottom of the screen. If you scroll down to the bottom, you will also see the top five recommended treasures for you to upgrade. Neat, huh? :)

Note: Your browser has no support for web storage. This means your data will not be saved when you close your browser or this tab.
